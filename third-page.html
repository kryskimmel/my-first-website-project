ABOUT US

CinderCobra is a project with a mission to collect and share artwork among all individuals. We are all artists, and CinderCobra aims to inspire all to bring their artistic visions to life and instill the philosophy that art is truly "in the eye of the beholder." The idea for this project began when the realization was made that artwork is seriously interesting. For instance, what was the artist thinking when they created their piece? What event inspired it? What emotions were they going through? What attitude did they hope viewers would have once they had glanced at their work? Is there a possibility that the artist did not have much thought at all as they were creating their work? There are so many questions that can come to mind just by taking the time to look at an artist's creation. Therefore, CinderCobra aspires to collect a variety of artwork of various mediums so that such works can be appreciated by others!
